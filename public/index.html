This is the guide page of Global Trading API, query in following format:
http://global-trading.josh-han.com/[export, import, all]/[5 digit country code]
http://global-trading.josh-han.com/[export, import, all]/[5 digit country code]/[6 digit product code]
e.g.
http://global-trading.josh-han.com/export/nausa
http://global-trading.josh-han.com/export/nausa/107283
